# Advisory Sales Motion

## Offer structure

| Step | Format | Price | When |
|------|--------|-------|------|
| Kickoff (standalone) | Pre-Mortem + Architecture Review, 2–3h | €800 | Entry point / decoupled first step |
| Full 2-Month Advisory | 8 weekly calls (60 min each) | €3,000 | After Kickoff or direct commitment |

- Kickoff is included in the full advisory (not additive)
- No automatic renewal — explicit check-in before any extension

## Sales motion

1. **Discovery calls** — identify constraint: founder on critical path, needs to step back by a deadline
2. **Send proposal** scoped to outcome ("off critical path by [date]")
3. **If prospect hesitates on full commitment** → offer Kickoff standalone as decoupled first step
4. **After Kickoff findings** → re-propose full advisory based on what emerged

## Common objections

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "I can't monitor/control ongoing" | Goal is internal ownership — train the right person inside, not add external oversight |
| "I need someone to train my team, not me" | Kickoff identifies the right internal person; separate coaching setup possible if needed |
| "Too much commitment upfront" | Decouple: Kickoff first (€800), then decide on the full engagement |
| "What about rituals / execution?" | Not part of advisory — thinking partner role only; can observe once for diagnostic feedback but not run them |

## Scoping rules

- **Ritual observation**: clarify which ritual before committing; not included in Kickoff by default; if agreed, it's a separate touchpoint
- **Team member evaluation** (e.g. is X the right person for role Y): natural output of Kickoff architecture review — include it
- **Execution / implementation**: separate engagement; client manages that relationship directly; Trung can point to people but does not manage them
- **Training the team directly**: not part of advisory; can design the setup and help find the right person

## What this is / is not

**Is:** Thinking partner, pre-mortem facilitator, decision architect, accountability container

**Is not:** Embedded operator, team manager, ritual facilitator, implementation lead
